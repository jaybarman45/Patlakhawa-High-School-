ABOUT US

PATLAKHAWA HIGH SCHOOL was established in 1973 and it is managed by the Department of Education. It is located in Rural area. It is located in COOCH BEHAR-II block of KOCH BIHAR district of West Bengal. The school consists of Grades from 5 to 12. The school is Co-educational and it doesn't have an attached pre-primary section. The school is N/A in nature and is not using school building as a shift-school. Bengali is the medium of instructions in this school. This school is approachable by all weather road. In this school academic session starts in April.
