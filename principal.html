Meet the Principal

Prabir Mitra,

The esteemed Head Sir of Patlakhawa High School (H.S.), is a distinguished educator. His leadership has been instrumental in shaping the school's ethos and vision.

With an M.A. and a B.Ed., he brings a wealth of academic and practical knowledge to our institution. His educational background is a testament to his dedication to learning and teaching.

Under his leadership, the school has flourished academically. Our students consistently achieve high marks, and the school's reputation for academic excellence continues to grow.

His dedication to fostering a nurturing environment is evident in his daily interactions with students and staff. He believes in creating a supportive atmosphere where everyone feels valued.

He emphasizes holistic education, balancing academics with extracurricular activities. This approach ensures that our students develop well-rounded personalities, equipped with various skills and talents.

His innovative teaching methods inspire both students and staff. He is always open to new ideas and encourages others to think creatively and critically.

A visionary leader, he continually strives for excellence. He sets high standards for himself and the school, always looking for ways to improve and innovate.His commitment to student success is unwavering. He works tirelessly to ensure that every student has the resources and support they need to succeed.

His approachable nature makes him a beloved figure among students, staff, and parents. He is always willing to listen and offer guidance, making everyone feel heard and respected.

We are proud to have Prabir Mitra at the helm of Patlakhawa High School. His leadership, dedication, and passion for education have made our school a beacon of learning and growth in the community.

Let's go!

Prabir Mitra

Head Sir
